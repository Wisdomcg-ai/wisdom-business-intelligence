## What shipped

### Engine extensions (additive, behind feature flag)

1. **New `CashflowEngineOptions` parameter** — optional, defaults preserve 28.0/28.1 behaviour exactly
   - `settings`: CashflowCalxaSettings (feature flag gate)
   - `xeroAccounts`: cached COA for account_id → code/name lookups
   - `capexByMonth`: per-month CapEx outflows

2. **Depreciation upgrade** (Phase 28.1 keyword → Phase 28.2 account ID)
   - When `settings.use_explicit_accounts=true`: match by configured `depreciation_expense_account_id`
   - Otherwise: falls back to keyword matching (current behaviour)

3. **Company Tax module** (`src/lib/cashflow/company-tax.ts`)
   - Annual tax = annual net profit × rate
   - Distributed across payment months per schedule
   - Supports `quarterly_payg_instalment`, `annual_aug`, `none` schedules
   - Appears in `liability_lines` as "Company Tax" on payment months

4. **CapEx module** (`src/app/api/forecast/cashflow/capex/route.ts`)
   - Fetches Xero balance sheet with `periods=N&timeframe=MONTH`
   - Identifies Fixed Asset accounts via `xero_accounts.xero_type = FIXED`
   - Computes month-over-month movement, reports positive diffs as CapEx
   - Engine adds to `asset_lines` as "CapEx (Fixed Assets)" when enabled

5. **Indirect-method output fields** on `CashflowForecastMonth`:
   - `net_profit` — Revenue - COGS - OpEx (accrual, per month)
   - `depreciation_addback` — non-cash add-back amount
   - `company_tax_payment` — tax paid this month
   - `capex_payment` — CapEx this month
   - All optional; direct-method fields unchanged

### Hook integration

`useCashflowForecast` now:
- Loads cashflow_settings + xero_accounts in parallel on mount
- Fetches CapEx data for the actuals period from `/api/forecast/cashflow/capex`
- Passes all three into `generateCashflowForecast(..., options)`
- Re-runs engine when settings or CapEx data change

## Impact on live clients

**Zero impact when feature flag is off** (the default):
- Depreciation keyword matching unchanged
- No company tax appears
- No CapEx lines appear
- Bank reconciliation works exactly as 28.1

**When `use_explicit_accounts=true` and settings configured**:
- Depreciation identified by explicit account (more reliable than keywords)
- Company tax starts appearing on BAS-quarter months
- CapEx starts appearing on months where Fixed Asset balance increased
- Net cash movement may DECREASE in those months (this is correct — CapEx and tax are real cash outflows the old model missed)

## Known limitations

- Company tax uses simple annual rate × profit (no YTD true-up, no prior-year instalments)
- CapEx captures NET movement in fixed assets (includes depreciation-driven decreases)
- Only 3 tax schedules supported hardcoded; more come in Phase 28.3
- Indirect-method UI rendering not yet built — fields are populated but not displayed (Phase 28.3 adds the toggle)
