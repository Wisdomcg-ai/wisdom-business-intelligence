## What shipped

### 3 algorithm fixes

1. **OpEx paid in accrual month** (`engine.ts` — OpEx loop)
   - Removed DPO timing from non-employment, non-bank-fee expenses
   - Per Calxa Rule 7: OpEx is paid immediately unless account has a per-account override (Phase 28.3)

2. **getTimingSplit rewritten** (`engine.ts:72-92`)
   - Old formula could produce >100% allocation for day ranges >30 (e.g., days=45 → 150% total)
   - New bucket-based formula guarantees `sum === 1.0` for any input
   - Exported for direct testing
   - Commit: `a59bd6f`

3. **Depreciation exclusion** (new `isDepreciationExpense()` helper + OpEx loop)
   - Depreciation and amortisation lines now skipped in OpEx cash flows
   - Keyword-based interim — Phase 28.2 upgrades to explicit account ID

## Impact on live clients

- **OpEx outflows shift earlier** in the month (correct behaviour per Calxa). Closing balances may drop slightly in early months but annual net is unchanged.
- **Depreciation no longer depresses bank balance** — clients with depreciation accounts (e.g. Dragon Roofing) will see higher bank balances in cashflow (correct).
- **DSO/DPO splits for days >30** now sum to 100% instead of 150% — may correct overestimated outflows for clients with long supplier payment terms.
- **Actual-month bank reconciliation to Xero (yesterday's fix) still works** — these changes only affect how cash receipts/payments are timed internally.
