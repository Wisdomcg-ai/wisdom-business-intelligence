A1 (Option 3 hybrid — chosen by Matt 2026-05-29 after wizard work in progress):

**Preserve** all existing `step_type='q1'`, `'q2'`, `'q3'`, `'q4'` rows for Armstrong — these are Matt's live wizard assignments (11 rows as of 2026-05-28T18:14 UTC).

**Dedupe** `strategic_initiatives` for Armstrong in TWO narrow operations only:

1. **Parking-lot dedupe**: delete `step_type='strategic_ideas'` rows whose normalized title (lowercased + trimmed) also exists as a `step_type='twelve_month'` row. Keep `strategic_ideas` rows with no `twelve_month` counterpart — those are the books and exploratory items (Atomic Habits, Wooden on Leadership, etc.).

2. **Cross-quarter duplicate cleanup**: if any initiative title appears in BOTH a Q1-Q4 step_type AND another Q1-Q4 step_type (i.e., assigned to two quarters at once), keep the row with the EARLIEST quarter (q1 > q2 > q3 > q4 priority) and delete the others. Specifically targets the "Set up a 6-12 month review of all suppliers - costs" duplicate found in both q2 and q4.

**Do NOT** apply any quarter assignment based on the 2026-05-12 transcript sequencing — that conflicts with Matt's live wizard work. Quarter assignment is now wizard-managed via `step_type`, not via the `quarter_assigned` column.

**Do NOT** touch `quarter_assigned`, `year_assigned`, `fiscal_year`, `start_date`, `end_date` columns on any row. The script leaves those untouched.

Purpose: Reduces 70-row inflation to ~52 rows (38 unique titles, 21 in twelve_month, ~11 quarter assignments, ~10 books/exploratory) without disturbing Matt's wizard state. Eliminates the data-inflation cause of the dedupe workaround at Step4AnnualPlan.tsx:360-368 for the parking-lot side without fighting the wizard.

Output: One Node script, dry-run by default, `--apply` writes.
</objective>
