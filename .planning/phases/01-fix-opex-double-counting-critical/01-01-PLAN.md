<objective>
Fix the core OpEx double-counting bug in the forecast calculation layer. Team cost lines (wages, salaries, superannuation, contractors) from Xero are currently summed in both Team Costs (Step 4) and OpEx, causing the BudgetTracker to show 461% utilization and Net Profit to be wildly wrong. This plan adds `isTeamCost()` guards to the two calculation files: `useForecastWizard.ts` (P&L summary) and `BudgetTracker.tsx` (budget tracker widget).

Purpose: Correct the financial calculations that every coach sees. This is the CRITICAL production bug.
Output: Two files with isTeamCost guards that exclude team cost lines from OpEx sums.
</objective>
