Trade-offs (D-44.2-13):
- standardLayout=true: Xero canonical 4-section tree (Income / Less Cost of Sales / Less Operating Expenses / Net Profit). Custom company sections flattened. Predictable section titles.
- standardLayout=false: company-customized tree as displayed in Xero web UI. Custom sub-sections like "Software Development - PK Costs" present. Tree depth varies by tenant. Parser parent-section carry-forward must handle nested sections.

The JDS classification bug (D-44.2-12) likely originates from standardLayout=false's nested section structure. The currentParentTitle carry-forward (pl-by-month-parser.ts:207-220) tries to inherit but fails on sub-section nodes that have a non-classifiable Title.
