## Architecture wins

1. **Path A per-month single-period queries.** Avoided the `?periods=N&timeframe=MONTH` pagination bug that returned 1000× wrong totals on multi-currency tenants (proven by IICT-HK by-month $-7,975 vs single-period $+1,432,486).
2. **Layout-vs-catalog discipline.** BS uses parser bucketing (matches Xero's web-rendered layout), P&L uses catalog `xero_type` (matches Xero's account-type taxonomy). Reversing this caused the JDS Mastercard $496 imbalance fixed in 06D.1.
3. **Per-tenant first-class.** sync_jobs writes one row per (business_id, tenant_id), `data_quality` rolls up worst-of, banner shows per-tenant breakdown.
4. **Continuous integrity, zero extra Xero quota.** 44.2-10 cron queries `sync_jobs.reconciliation` instead of re-fetching from Xero — covers 18 tenants for the cost of one DB query, drift caught within one sync cycle.
5. **Allow-list discipline.** Two real Xero quirks (JDS Rent annual accrual; IICT-HK FX revaluation) handled via test-harness allow-list with justification, not by masking.
